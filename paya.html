Paya Nihari

Ingredients:

  • Goats trotters ( paya ) 18
  • Onion 1 finely slices, Green chilles 8-9
  • Ginger-garlic paste 2 Tbspns
  • Kashmiri red chilli powder 2 Tspns
  • Turmeric Powder 1⁄2 Tspn
  • Mint leaves 1⁄2 bunch
  • Cilantro/Coriander leaves 1 bunch & Salt to taste
  • Red chilli powder 1Tspn (add more if u want it to be spicy)
  • All spice (Garam masala) powder 1 Tspn
  • Wheat flour 2 handfuls
  • Potli ka masala (1 packet) or nihari or paya masala 2 Tbspns
  • Fresh ginger pieces (1" inch)
  • Oil for cooking

Directions:

  1. Pressure cook the trotters with all ingredients and enough water for 40 minutes along with potli ka masala till tender ( tie your potli ka masala in a small muslin cloth) or You can cook on low flame in a vessel till the meat condenses ( keep adding water if the water evaporates).
  2. Now heat about 4-5 Tbspns of oil in a big vessel.
  3. Add the sliced onions along with green chilles , When the onions turn golden brown in color add the ginger-garlic paste along with fresh ginger pieces.
  4. Also add red chilli powder , turmeric powder, salt, all spice powder.
  5. Now add wheat flour and fry well till it gets properly roasted. Cook on low flame or you will burn your masalas.
  6. Add condensed meat along with its soup. Also add nihari masala/paya masla if u didn't add your potli ka masla in the step 1. Add enough water so that the consistency of the nihari is soupy. Add coriander and mint leaves and boil for some time.

    7. Difficulty Level: Medium  |  Cooking time: 1 hour  |  Servings: 4-5
