Khichdi Khatta

Ingredients:

  • 1⁄2 kg Rice either basmati or any good quality
  • 3⁄4 cup Red lentils or masoor dal
  • 1 tbsp Ginger garlic paste
  • 3 tbsp Ghee
  • 1 tsp Caraway seeds
  • 2-3 Cinnamon sticks
  • 3-4 Cardamom pods crushed, Salt to taste
  • 4 Green chillies whole
  • 1 tsp Garam masala powder
  • 1⁄2 cup Coriander and mint leaves chopped
  • 1 tsp Turmeric powder
  • 1 onion sliced

    • For the raw imli and til ka khatta:

  • 1 cup Water
  • 1 lemon sized tamarind ball
  • 4 tbsp Roasted and powdered sesame seeds
  • 4 Green chillies slit
  • 1 large onion thinly sliced
  • 1⁄4 cup chopped coriander leaves
  • 1 tsp Salt

Directions:

  1. First of all, soak rice and the lentils after washing thoroughly for at least 20 minutes. Now in a non-stick pot, heat ghee and add the ginger garlic paste and the sliced onion to it. Saute it for a few minutes until the raw smell fades away on a low heat.
  2. Add the whole garam masalas one by one. Also add the garam masala powder, turmeric and the green chilies and coriander leaves. After about 5 minutes of sauteeing, add the soaked rice and lentils.
  3. Saute for a minute and then add about 4 cups of water along with the salt. For the first 10 minutes, let it cook on high flame stirring in between, to mix everything well. After 10 minutes, lower the flame and cover.
  4. Let it cook on low flame until done. It will be done in 18-20 minutes. Keep mixing in between with light hands just to ensure that it is mixed up well.
  5. FOR THE RAW IMLI AND TIL KA KHATTA.....Squeeze the soaked tamarind well to take out the juice. To this add the salt and the roasted sesame seeds powder. Also, add the sliced onion and the slit green chillies.
  6. And finally, garnish it with chopped coriander leaves. Khatta is always of a thin consistency only. It tastes awesome. Serve the delicious khichdi with this khatta, kheema, pickle of your choice, poppadums and any fried item. Sesame seeds powder can be added more if desired to make a thicker consistency khatta.

    7. Difficulty Level: Medium  |  Cooking time: 30 minutes  |  Servings: 4-5
